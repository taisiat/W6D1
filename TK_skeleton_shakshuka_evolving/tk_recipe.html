Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 bell peppers (any color), chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 teaspoons of ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 28 oz. can of crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 4 large eggs
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped (optional)
  • Crusty bread, for serving

Shakshuka a la TK

Description

Shakshuka is a Middle Eastern dish made with eggs cooked in a flavorful tomato and pepper sauce. Here's a simple recipe for making shakshuka.

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and bell peppers and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using) and cook for another minute.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and season with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer and let cook for 10 minutes, until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Use a spoon to make four wells in the sauce. Crack an egg into each well.
  6. Cover the skillet with a lid and cook for about 5 minutes, or until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still runny.
  7. Sprinkle with chopped parsley or cilantro (if using). Serve hot with crusty bread on the side. Enjoy!
